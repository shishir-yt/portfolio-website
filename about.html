About


UX Designer based in Nepal. Who loves designing products that are simple, intuitive and helpful.

Expanding and adapting my thought, skillset and processes to the changing problems and needs in the world around us.

My design practice so far includes product/visual design, research and branding. I also enjoy film-making, and illustration.

I seek brave teams where, together, we can create lasting and meaningful impact on people's lives, the society, and the world we live in.

My inspiration comes from video games, movies, music, people and situations I see in my everyday life.
