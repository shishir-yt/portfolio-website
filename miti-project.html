Miti App

A Minimal calendar application

Client

Skybase Innovations

Date

20 Nov 2022

Category

UI/UX

Miti is a minimal calendar app that makes scheduling and planning easier than ever. Miti's design is based on a minimalist concept.

It has a simple, straightforward, and user-friendly interface. Miti's beauty is that it is designed to be adaptable and customizable. You can change the app's appearance and feel. Working on this project was a lot of fun, and I loved every minute of it.
