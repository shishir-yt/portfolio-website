The cover art for FIFA 23 is a fun and creative design that features a photo of KSI and iShowSpeed from the recent Sidemen charity football match.

The design incorporates elements from the FIFA game, such as the logo and the football pitch, to create a unique and eye-catching design. The amusing expressions of KSI and iShowSpeed add a sense of playfulness and energy to the design.
