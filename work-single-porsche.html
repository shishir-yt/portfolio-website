Porsche | Re-design


The Porsche Models page re-design is aimed to revamp the user interface and visual design of the models page for the renowned car brand, Porsche.



What I did?


The vibrant red background is strategically chosen to evoke emotions, reinforce brand identity, establish visual hierarchy, and ensure optimal contrast and readability.

Through this captivating backdrop, users are engaged and guided to explore and appreciate the range of Porsche vehicles, creating a cohesive and memorable user experience.
