About Me

Designer/Developer


Hey! My name is Shishir. I work as a UI/UX designer and developer in Pokhara, Nepal. When I discovered user interface design during my freshman year of college, I knew it was the right fit for me. I could design with my artistic side and code with my rational side. As an added plus, because I am both a designer and a developer, I can ensure that no detail is lost in translation.
