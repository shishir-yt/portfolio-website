My Design Process



1. Brief

We spend some time getting to know you and your needs during the brief. This will enable me to develop a product that benefits you, your company, and your target market.


2. Research

In this phase, I'll get to know your target market, your users, the contexts in which they'll use your product, and the needs they'll be trying to satisfy.


3. Design

I begin formulating ideas using what I've learned about you and your audience. The user flows and sitemap for the product come first, then wireframing. I'll get feedback as we develop our concepts so that we can make adjustments as we go.


4. Test and Iterate

I make sure the prototype satisfies user expectations and the product achieves initial business objectives through numerous testing and iteration rounds.
