Health Reminder | Valorant


The in-game alert is focused on promoting a healthier gaming habit and overall well-being among players.



Why?


The message, "Rest and recharge," is displayed in a visually appealing and non-intrusive manner, ensuring that players receive the message without feeling forced or interrupted.

Through this design, players are empowered to adopt a balanced approach to gaming, recognizing the significance of self-care and overall well-being. The Valorant in-game alert serves as a reminder and support system, fostering a healthier gaming environment for players.
